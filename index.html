Chi siamo

Innovative Delivery Solutions: F.E.D.D. is a Srl founded by four students from ISII Marconi in Piacenza. Our company specializes in drone delivery services for e-commerce platforms, offering efficient, eco-friendly, and high-quality delivery solutions with zero emissions.

  • Giovanni De Simone: Responsabile alle risorse umane, Locator.
